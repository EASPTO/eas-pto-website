Meetings

How the EAS PTO stays connected and keeps things running.

Board Meetings

The EAS PTO Board meets once a month to discuss PTO-related business, review financials, and plan upcoming programs and events.

As a PTO member, you can request information from any board meeting by emailing president@easpto.org.

General Membership Meetings

General Meetings for all EAS PTO members are held twice a year:

📍 Fall: Typically at EAS Curriculum Night

📍 Spring: At the Celebration of Student Work in June

Any important PTO general membership votes will occur at one of these two meetings. All PTO members are encouraged to attend.

Meeting Minutes

Meeting minutes are available to PTO members upon request. Email president@easpto.org to request copies of recent board meeting minutes.

Not a member yet?

Join the PTO to get involved and have a voice in how we support EAS. Membership forms are available on the About page or in the PTO Basket in the EAS office.
