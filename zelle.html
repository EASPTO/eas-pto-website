How to Pay by Zelle

Fast, free, and no checks required. Send payments directly to EAS PTO using your bank's app or website.

treasurer@easpto.org

What can I pay by Zelle?

Zelle can only be used for payments made directly to EAS PTO. Some fees go directly to the school and still require cash or check.

Pay EAS PTO via Zelle

  • PTO Membership
  • Family Contribution
  • Wednesday Electives
  • Yearbooks
  • Bingo / Student Socials

Must pay school by cash/check

  • Camps
  • Classroom fees
  • April Adventures
  • Spring trips

General steps (all banks)

  1. Open your bank's online banking website or mobile app and find the Zelle tab or section. Most major banks have it built in.
  2. Add EAS PTO as a new payee. Use the email treasurer@easpto.org. Set the type to Personal, first name EAS, last name PTO. The email is the most important part — get that right and you're set.
  3. Once the payee is saved, select EAS PTO and enter the payment amount.
  4. In the memo or description field, include your student's name and what you're paying for — for example: "Jane Smith, Session 1 Elective fee." This helps our Treasurer reconcile payments.
  5. Submit. Done! You'll get a confirmation from your bank.
Important: If your bank account is not registered under one of the parent names on file with EAS, it can be hard to match your payment to the right student. If that's the case, email treasurer@easpto.org with a screenshot of the transaction and a note about which student you're paying for.

Step-by-step by bank

Select your bank for specific instructions.

🏦 Wells Fargo

  1. Log into Wells Fargo online banking or the mobile app.
  2. Navigate to Pay & Transfer → Zelle.
  3. Select Add a New Recipient.
  4. Enter: First name EAS, Last name PTO. Under "Send by," choose Email address and enter treasurer@easpto.org.
  5. Click Save & Send Money to add EAS PTO to your Zelle contacts.
  6. Select EAS PTO, enter your amount, add your student's name and payment description in the memo, and send.

🏦 Chase

  1. Log into Chase online banking or the mobile app.
  2. Go to Pay & Transfer → Pay bills & send money with Zelle.
  3. Click Manage Recipients → Add a recipient.
  4. Enter name EAS, then under Email enter treasurer@easpto.org and confirm it. Click Save.
  5. Once saved, select EAS PTO from your recipients. You'll see it confirmed as "Registered as ENVIRONMENTAL AND ADVENTURE SCHOOL PTO."
  6. Enter your payment amount, add your student's name and what you're paying for in the Memo field, and click Next to send.

🏦 Bank of America

  1. Log into Bank of America online banking or the mobile app.
  2. Go to the Zelle section (usually under Transfers or Bill Pay).
  3. Click Add a new Recipient. Select Individual as the recipient type.
  4. Enter treasurer@easpto.org as the email address and confirm it. Click Add Recipient.
  5. Select EAS PTO from your recipients. Enter your payment amount.
  6. Add your student's name and payment description in the Message (optional) field, then click Continue transfer to send.

🏦 BECU

  1. Log into BECU online banking or the mobile app.
  2. Go to the Payments tab and select Send Money with Zelle. If you haven't used Zelle before, you'll need to sign up first — just follow the prompts.
  3. Once enrolled, go to Add New Contact. Select Personal.
  4. Enter First name EAS, Last name PTO. Under the contact method, select Email and enter treasurer@easpto.org. Click Save.
  5. Select EAS PTO (it will show as "Enrolled as ENVIRONMENTAL AND ADVENTURE SCHOOL PTO"). Enter your amount.
  6. Include your student's name and payment details, then click Review and confirm to send.

🏦 Other Banks & Credit Unions

Most banks with Zelle follow the same general flow. Here's what to look for:

  1. Find Zelle in your bank's app or website — usually under "Transfers," "Payments," or "Send Money."
  2. Add a new recipient using the email treasurer@easpto.org. Use first name EAS, last name PTO.
  3. Enter your payment amount and include your student's name and what you're paying for in the memo or description field.
  4. Submit. Your bank will confirm the payment was sent.

Don't see your bank listed? Most banks that offer Zelle use a nearly identical process. If you get stuck, contact our Treasurer at treasurer@easpto.org.

Prefer to pay by check or cash?

No problem! Cash and checks are always accepted. Drop them off at the EAS Office during school hours. Make checks payable to EAS PTO.

We strongly prefer Zelle when possible — it saves time and makes life easier for our Treasurer — but we will never turn away a check.
