Payment Policy

Unless otherwise noted, all electives and PTO program fees must be submitted to the EAS PTO. We prefer payments via Zelle. Cash and checks can be dropped off in the EAS Office.

Pay by Zelle

⚡ Fast, free, and easy

EAS PTO is enrolled in Zelle to make it easy for families to pay electronically. Zelle is offered by most banks and credit unions at no charge — no transaction fees for you or for us.

Zelle to: treasurer@easpto.org

→ View full Zelle instructions with bank-specific steps

  1. Open your bank's online banking site or mobile app and go to the Zelle tab or section.
  2. Add EAS PTO as a payee using the email treasurer@easpto.org. Set the type to Personal, first name EAS, last name PTO. The email address is the most important part.
  3. Once the payee is added, select EAS PTO and enter the payment amount.
  4. You can add something to the comment/memo box for your own tracking — but note that comments do not come through to the Treasurer, so don't rely on them for communication.
  5. Submit your payment. You're done!

Heads up: If your bank account is not registered under one of the parent names on file with EAS, it can be hard for our Treasurer to match your payment to the right student. If that's the case, please email treasurer@easpto.org with a screenshot of the transaction and a note about which student you're paying for.

Questions? Contact our Treasurer at treasurer@easpto.org.

What Can I Pay by Zelle?

Zelle can only be used for payments made directly to EAS PTO. Some fees go directly to the school and still require cash or check — Zelle cannot be used for those.

  • Bingo
  • Student Socials

Pay school by cash or check

  • Camps
  • Classroom fees
  • April Adventures
  • Spring trips

Pay by Check or Cash

🏫 Drop off at the EAS Office

Cash and checks are accepted and can be dropped off at the EAS Office during school hours. Make checks payable to EAS PTO.

We encourage Zelle when possible — it saves time and makes reconciliation much easier for our Treasurer — but we will always accept cash and checks.

Financial Assistance

We believe every EAS student should be able to participate fully in all programs, regardless of financial circumstances. If your family needs support covering program fees, please reach out confidentially to treasurer@easpto.org.

You can also visit the EAS school site for district-level financial assistance information.
