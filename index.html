Welcome, EAS Families!

The EAS PTO exists to support the students, staff, and programs that make our school so incredible. We look forward to helping EAS have a successful and rewarding school year. Explore our site to learn how to get involved, donate, or pay for electives and programs.

What We Do

🏕️

Camps & Adventures

EAS students attend three week-long camping and adventure experiences each year, building independence and community in the outdoors.

Learn more →
🌱

Wednesday Electives

Five-to-six week elective sessions give students the chance to explore something new. All elective payments are processed through the PTO.

Pay for electives →
🤝

Community Service

All EAS students participate in two 4-day Community Stewardship Project blocks each year, engaging in environmental and social stewardship.

Learn more →
💛

Staff Appreciation

We support "Monthly Meals" for our amazing EAS staff and host special appreciation events throughout the year.

See staff favorites →
🎉

Events

PTO organizes the End of Year Celebration, 8th grade dinner/dance, 8th grade graduation, and community-building family events.

Learn more →

Health Fair

The EAS Health Fair is an enriching 3-day conference that fulfills district and state Health Curriculum guidelines for all students.
