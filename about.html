About the EAS PTO

Who we are, what we stand for, and how we're organized.

Our Mission

  • To promote the participation of parents in the educational process.
  • To promote communication between teachers, parents, and students.
  • To generate financial support for educational programs.
  • To provide opportunities for student involvement in the community.
  • To enhance the educational experience through environmental awareness and adventure learning opportunities.

Join the PTO
